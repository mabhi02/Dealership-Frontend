What is The Dealership?

In today's heavily virtual world, everything we do is steadily being transferred online. Whether that would be banking for crypto currency, playing for recreation, or shopping online; the fact of that matter is that's its much more efficient to do these tasks online now. The Dealership aims to provide a resource for companies, specifically in the auto-industry, to adapt to the status quo and reach new heights through this client-driven visions.

This project boastes our hand-crafted car visualizer, in this case, with the company Tesla. Users can select differnt Tesla models, colors, and aesthetics, all while manging the car to meet their budget. All in all, The Dealership gives a potenial client both an incredible and easy to user interface while keeping the clients priorities of budgets features first. This project was built with React.JS, Node.JS, HTML, CSS, and Bootsrap all hosted on Netlify and Heroku servers.
